How to play

Make your choice

Choose your favourite theme and how difficult you want the game to be.

Match cards

Flip the cards and find matching pairs. Matches are removed from the game board.

Moves and scoring

Each attempt to find matching pairs counts as one move. Moves are tracked and players are scored based on the number of moves.

Timer

A timer starts when the game begins, tracking how long the player takes to match all pairs.

End of game

The game ends when all card pairs are matched. Players receive their scores and time.
